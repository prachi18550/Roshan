INTRODUCTION:-

Hi, This is Prachi Gupta , a highschool student of Delhi Public School Mathura Road. In today’s fast-changing world, too many talented individuals find themselves underemployed — working in jobs that don’t fully utilize their skills, education, or experience. At ROSHAN , we understand how frustrating and discouraging that can be. That’s why we’ve created this platform: to be a guiding hand for people who are ready to take control of their careers, grow their skillsets, and find opportunities that truly align with their strengths and aspirations.

Our mission is to help underemployed individuals recognize their potential and turn it into progress. Whether you're a recent graduate struggling to break into your field, someone re-entering the workforce, or a professional looking to transition into a more fulfilling role, we’re here for you.

On this site, you’ll find a variety of tools and resources tailored to your journey — including personalized skill-building programs, resume and interview guidance, mentorship opportunities, and curated job listings based on your background and interests.

More than just a job board or a training site, ROSHAN is a supportive community that believes in second chances, untapped potential, and the power of knowledge. Our goal is simple: to bridge the gap between where you are and where you want to be.

Let us help you take the next step — because meaningful work should be within everyone's reach.
